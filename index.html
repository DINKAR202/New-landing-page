Liberal Arts Courses - An Introduction

        Liberal arts courses prepare students to deal with the complexities of today's modern world. Students acquire a broad understanding of the world from a multidisciplinary concept that includes social and environmental sciences, society, and culture, including in-depth education in their desired field. Liberal Arts covers a broad range of disciplines that includes humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and mathematics.

Why Choose Liberal Arts in 2023?

  • Liberal arts courses offer a multidisciplinary approach to learning.
  • Liberal arts graduates can work in various areas such as journalism, public policy, education, etc.
  • Liberal Arts courses equip students with the skills to solve issues, think creatively, and come up with unique ideologies.

Courses

UG Courses

B.A Economics

B.A Economics is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on studying economic theory, policy, and analysis. It covers various topics such as microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, and international economics.

B.A.(Hons.) Liberal Arts

B.A. (Hons.) Liberal Arts & Humanities is a 4-year undergraduate program in India that includes studies of liberal arts and humanities such as literature, philosophy, history, languages, and cultures.

B.A.(Hons.) Sociology

B.A. (Hons) in Sociology is a 4-year undergraduate program in India that includes studies of human society and social relationships, develops critical thinking and research skills, and opens up career opportunities in fields such as research, social work, academics.

B.A.(Hons.) Fine Arts

B.A. (Hons) Fine Arts is a 4-year undergraduate program that includes studies of visual arts such as painting, sculpture, graphic design, and illustration. It includes technical skills, techniques, art history, and theory.

B.A Hindi

A BA in Hindi is a 3-year creative journey that unveils the beauty of the Hindi language, literature, and culture. You will explore grammar, literature, linguistics, and related subjects.

B.A Geography

BA in Geography is a 3-year journey that unveils India's landscapes and the forces that shape them. You will discover the earth's physical features and human activities. With a unique blend of theory and practical .

B.A History

BA in History is a 3-year adventure through India's rich past, from ancient civilizations to modern times. You will explore the people, events, and movements that have shaped our world, delving into the study of political, economic, social, cultural, and intellectual history.

B.A Political Science

BA in Political Science in India is a 3-year undergraduate program that provides a comprehensive understanding of politics and government. It covers topics such as international relations, public administration, political theory, comparative politics, and Indian government and politics.s.

B.A Mathematics

BA in Mathematics is a 3-year undergraduate degree in India combining math and related subjects. The curriculum covers topics like calculus, linear algebra, and statistics.

B.A Physical Education

BA in Physical Education is a 3-year undergraduate degree that delivers the study of human movement, sports training, and physical education.

B.A Media Studies

BA in Media Studies delivers the study of Journalism, Filmmaking, AVl Production, Digital Communication, International Communication, Media Design, Storytelling, and Digital Art.

Post-Graduate Courses

M.A.(Economics)

M.A. Economics is a 2-year postgraduate degree in India that covers micro and macroeconomic theories, policies, and analysis.

M.A.(Hons.) Liberal Arts

M.A. (Hons.) Liberal Arts & Humanities is a 2-year postgraduate program in India that covers various subjects of liberal arts and humanities and develops critical thinking, analytical, and research skills.

M.A.(Hons.) Sociology

M.A. (Hons) in Sociology is a 2-year postgraduate program in India that studies human society and social relationships, develops critical thinking and research skills, and opens up career opportunities in fields such as research, social work, academics.

M.A.(Hons.) Fine Arts

Master of Fine Arts is a graduate program designed to immerse students in the fine arts, including visual arts, creative writing, film, and theater.

M.A.(English)

MA in English is a 2-year postgraduate degree program in India that focuses on studying English literature, language, and culture. It covers courses in classical and contemporary literature, language studies, literary criticism, creative writing, and research methodology.

M.A.(Mathematics)

M.A. Mathematics is a 2-year postgraduate program that covers advanced topics in mathematics with applications in various fields and includes a combination of lectures, discussions, assignments, and research projects.

M.A.(Hindi)

M.A. Hindi is a 2-year postgraduate program focusing on the Hindi language, literature, and culture and improves fluency and proficiency in speaking, reading, and writing Hindi.

M.A.(History)

MA in History in India is a 2-year post-grad program. The Curriculum is diverse and covers topics such as ancient, medieval, modern, and Indian history, world history, and historiography.

M.A.(Geography)

MA in Geography is a 2-year undergraduate program in India. It covers physical and human geography, including geomorphology, remote sensing, and cartography, and includes a research project or internship for practical experience.

M.A.(Political Science)

MA in Political Science is a 2-year post-grad program covering political systems, theories, processes, comparative politics, international relations, political theory, public administration, and research methods.

M.A.(Physical Education)

MA in Physical Education is a 2-year post-grad program studying physical education and sports science, including exercise physiology, sports psychology, sports sociology, and sports medicine.

Eligibility Criteria

Under-Graduate Courses

The Students must have completed 10+2/equivalent in any discipline from a recognized board/University with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.

Some Colleges/University requires students to appear for Entrance Exam.

Post-Graduate Courses

The Students must obtain a bachelor's degree in Liberal Arts from a recognized College/University.

Some Colleges/University requires students to appear for Entrance Exam.
